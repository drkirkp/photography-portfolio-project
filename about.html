About me

Dail Williams

Born and raised in rural Illinois, Dail found her passion for photography early in life, as with many of her creative endeavors. She moved to Chicago in 2011 and loves to travel the world with her camera (and her husband). I hope to evoke a sense of wonder and reverence for the natural world and historical sites, inviting viewers to pause, reflect, and appreciate the beauty that surrounds them.
